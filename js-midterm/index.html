JS Project - Phone Number

Instructions: Use the "Down Arrow" key to decrement the counter.
Once the desired phone number has been reached, click "Phone Number Located".
If you have gone past the desired phone number, you must hit the override button to reenter the number.
Don't run out of time! Spam the "Space Bar" key to give yourself more time.
You cannot decrement the phone number and increment the time simultaneously.
Good luck.
